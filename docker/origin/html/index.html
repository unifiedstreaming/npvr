Unified Remix nPVR

This demo showcases an nPVR workflow that uses Unified Remix to generate on-the-fly VOD clips from an archive that was captured to disk by Unified Capture, from a Unified Origin live stream.

The benefits of this solution:

  • Allows your customers to make personal recordings from your live stream
  • Ensures complete flexibility because clips are generated on-the-fly
  • Everything is stored on disk only once, guaranteeing maximum efficiency
  • Makes it possible to keep an infinite archive of your live stream
  • Streams in all formats to all devices, from a single source
